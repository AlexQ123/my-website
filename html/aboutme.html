Beethoven ft. Drake

Unfortunately, there's not a song out there where both of these artists hop on the track. If there was, I'd probably like it. I love music! I've been playing the piano for 13 years now, and even though I've had less time to tickle the ivories recently, I cherish the moments when I get to. I played a lot of classical, romantic, baroque, and contemporary music, all of which I like to listen to as well. I've entered quite a few piano competitions, placing 1st in multiple Encore Keyboard competitions and the Salt Lake Piano competition. I even got to perform in Carnegie Hall in 2015 after placing 2nd in the American Protégé International Music Competition. More recently, I've performed with the Utah Philharmonic Orchestra and the Utah Valley Symphony twice. Check out this video of me playing with the Utah Valley Symphony!


After seeing that video, people think there's no way that I could ever like rap. Well guess what? I do. In fact, I also like pop, indie, rock, country, and oldies. I love making music and listening to music, and I do these things a lot.
